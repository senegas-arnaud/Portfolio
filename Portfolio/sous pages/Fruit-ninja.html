Pygame Fruits Ninja

Fruits Ninja


User have to slash fruit using is keyboard. Your score grow with the number of fruits slashing. Cutting an ice cube froze the board and allow you to cut all the fruits during this time. Missing 3 fruits or cutting a bomb is an instant game over. You can choose difficulty at the beginning and enter your name and register as a player at the end of the game.
